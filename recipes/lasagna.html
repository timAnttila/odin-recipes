Homemade Lasagna

This is my mom's special homemade lasagna recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling.

Ingredients

  • ½ pound ground pork
  • ½ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ⅛ teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese

How to

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the ground pork and lean ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Stir in minced onion and crushed garlic; cook until softened.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, chopped fresh parsley (reserve some for garnish), dried basil, 1 ½ teaspoons salt, dried oregano, and white sugar. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Boil lasagna noodles according to package directions until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  6. In a bowl, mix cottage cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, eggs, 2 teaspoons salt, and ground black pepper until smooth.
  7. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  8. Layer noodles over the sauce.
  9. Spread a portion of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  10. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the cheese mixture.
  11. Repeat layering sauce, noodles, cheese mixture, and mozzarella until ingredients are used, ending with meat sauce topped with mozzarella.
  12. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove foil and bake an additional 25 minutes, until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  14. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing.
  15. Garnish with remaining chopped parsley and serve warm.
